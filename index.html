This is your life.

Pushing D on your keyboard represents the effort you’re willing to put in.

You cannot control age — time moves on regardless. Difficulty changes with age: some seasons bring health and fewer problems; later seasons bring more problems and less strength.

The more consistent your effort, the more likely you are to rise above the middle — with better stability, options, and care. If you stop moving, you slide toward the bottom. Even if you push hard, everyone dies; the journey changes, not the ending.

When you are in your prime (20–50) it’s the best time to climb the success ladder. If you miss that period or start close to the end of your prime, you need to face a harsh truth — it will not be easy, and you will feel it in the game, it will become harder. So do not waste your time!
